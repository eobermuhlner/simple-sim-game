List of 64x64 map objects for this medieval sim game.

The list entries are in the form
- file-name: description

The file-name is the systematic name that will be used in the game, without .png

# Terrain Objects

Terrain objects are natural decorations spawned based on terrain type.

| Object | Valid Terrain | Spawn Rate |
|--------|--------------|------------|
| tree-large | Grass, Forest | 50% on Grass, 80% on Forest |
| tree-small | Grass | 50% on Grass |
| boulder-large | Grass, Forest | 10% on Grass, 20% on Forest |
| boulder-small | Grass, Stone | 40% on Stone |
| boulder-large-snow | Snow | 20% on Snow |

## Object Sprites

- tree-large: Large tree canopy viewed from top-down
- tree-small: Small tree canopy viewed from top-down
- boulder-large: Large grey boulder viewed from top-down
- boulder-small: Small grey boulder viewed from top-down
- boulder-large-snow: Large grey boulder with snow on top

# Roads

Roads improve travel speed and caravan capacity. Higher tier roads enable faster transport and more caravans.

| Road Type | Speed Mult | Capacity | Upkeep | Tech Required |
|-----------|------------|----------|--------|---------------|
| dirt | 1.0x | 2 | None | From start |
| stone | 1.5x | 4 | Low | Mining Town |
| cobblestone | 2.0x | 6 | Medium | Mining Town (adv) |
| roman | 3.0x | 10 | High | Trade Hub |

## Road Sprites

Roads are rendered as 64x64 tiles with directional connections.
Use road tileset with variants for straight, corner, T-junction, and crossroad pieces.

- road-dirt: Dirt road with tire tracks
- road-stone: Stone paved road
- road-cobblestone: Cobblestone road with small stones
- road-roman: Roman-style paved road with gutters

# Object Naming Convention

- Terrain objects: `tree-large`, `boulder-small`, etc.
- Buildings: `house-simple`, `market-large`, etc.
- Roads: `road-dirt`, `road-stone`, etc.
- Sea: `ship-small`, `harbor-large`, etc.
- Bridges: `bridge-wood`, `bridge-stone`, etc.

All sprites: 64x64 pixels, top-down view, medieval style, consistent pixel art aesthetic.
